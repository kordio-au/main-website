About Kordio

Making Enterprise AI
Accessible to Everyone

We're on a mission to give every small business access to the same AI technology that powers the world's biggest companies.

Our Mission

We Believe Every Business Deserves Enterprise-Grade AI

Most AI tools are built for big companies with big budgets. We think that's wrong. The local pizza shop, the plumber around the corner, the family dental practice — they all deserve technology that helps them compete and grow.

That's why we build AI solutions that are affordable, easy to set up, and purpose-built for the way real small businesses actually operate. No jargon, no complexity — just tools that work.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Australian Made

Built and supported by a local team in Australia. We understand the market because we're part of it. Your data stays local, your support is a phone call away.

Customer First

Every feature we build starts with a real problem a real business owner told us about. We don't build technology for technology's sake — we build solutions that deliver outcomes.

Simplicity

If it's not easy to use, it's not good enough. Our products are designed for business owners, not developers. Set up in minutes, not weeks. No technical knowledge required.
